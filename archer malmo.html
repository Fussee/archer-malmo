Archer Malmo Experience

Start of the Day

The start of the day was like any other day. After going outside the weather was pretty warm. Taking about 20 minutes to get there when we left the school. With everyone on the bus being "hyped" to go to Archer & Malmo. People being restless with talks of their expectation of the place.

When arriving there

When we arrived it was on a kind of sketchy street, but when entering into the building the entrance was a cotton museum. The elevators were nice but the it would have been faster to take the stairs to be honest. The building seem warm and welcoming during that cold day.

Talk

While it was a business like environment it also had a joke like environment. There was joke on the board talking about what to do with the empty space. They take things seriously when it needs to be, but also had time to have fun which is great in that environment. The powerpoint was somewhat lengthy but besides that it was memorable.

Tour

I would have thought that the main lobby for Archer and Malmo would have been closer to ground floor. There was some cubical and some more spacious areas in the building. Instead of the plain colors in a office job it was decently bright and pleasant to the eyes. I had expected this place to be some what busy and have stacks of papers up on some of the desk but it was decently organised.

Eating/End of Day

After being shown around Archer & Malmo it was time to leave. Leaving around 12:00. We get back on the bus and headed to CookOut and we get ourself some decent for $6 with some milkshake. With nothing else we joked about some intresting thing on the way back, but honestly everyone was tired out so there was not too many people talking. When we got back to school the school just seem so plain compared to Archer & Malmo. Well if you have time inspect element; there is something cool there.
